About VTH Supply Chain Solutions

We are proud to introduce a new division: VTH GREEN. With our existing national footprint and a fleet of over 150 diesel vehicles, VTH GREEN is committed to minimizing our carbon footprint while supporting our customers' sustainability ambitions.

VTH GREEN aims to pioneer the Outside Hire industry by complementing our fleet with an electric vehicle (EV) range. Our goal is to accelerate the transition toward a more eco-friendly transportation landscape through collaboration with like-minded partners and stakeholders.

Driving Sustainability

We are dedicated to making a positive impact in the communities we serve. A key part of this commitment is our investment in a cleaner transport future, as we actively pioneer the integration of electric trucks into our Australian fleet. We believe in driving change, not just cargo, and are proud to be at the forefront of a more sustainable logistics industry.

  • Smart Fleet & Logistics

    Optimizing every journey to reduce our carbon footprint.

  • Green Warehousing

    Efficient and eco-friendly storage and distribution hubs.

  • Eco-Friendly Packaging

    Reducing waste from source to destination.

Optimizing Our Fleet

  • Transitioning to a fully electric vehicle (EV) fleet for last-mile deliveries.
  • Using AI-powered route optimization to reduce mileage and fuel consumption by 15%.
  • Implementing regular, proactive vehicle maintenance to ensure peak fuel efficiency.

Innovating Our Hubs

  • Installing solar panels on all major warehousing facilities to generate clean energy.
  • Utilizing automated sorting systems to reduce energy consumption per parcel.
  • Implementing advanced water recycling and rainwater harvesting programs.

Rethinking Packaging

  • Using satchels and boxes made from 100% recycled and recyclable materials.
  • Offering incentives for customers who opt into our minimal packaging program.
  • Partnering exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability.
